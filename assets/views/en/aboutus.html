What Instaraising is

Instaraising is a fundraising portal with an intuitive search, making it easy to match your passions with charitable organizations. With just a few clicks, you can find and support causes that engage you.

Who We Are

Today, 670 million people globally live in extreme poverty, earning less than 2 USD a day. Our greatest aspiration at Instaraising is to reduce this number by five percent, helping 35 million people escape extreme poverty. Our goal is ambitious, but the need is great. From providing emergency aid in crisis situations and social work to improve lives, to protecting nature and fostering a sustainable future – the range of causes is vast. That’s why we created Instaraising, a portal uniting all charity foundations in one service. We simplify the process of finding and supporting organizations aligned with your values. Our commitment is strong – ensuring your contributions are effectively utilized for their intended purposes. We collaborate exclusively with organizations approved by reputable financial oversight authorities.
