Frequently Asked Questions

Instaraising is a digital platform designed to connect charity donors with charitable organizations globally. Our mission is to facilitate effective giving and make a substantial impact in reducing global extreme poverty.

Instaraising allows donors to discover and connect with various charitable organizations and causes. Donors can directly fund projects or causes they care about, and organizations can showcase their initiatives and receive support through our official website.

We support a wide range of causes including crisis management, humanitarian aid, education, healthcare, community development, children’s welfare, human rights, environmental conservation, and many more.

Instaraising is unique in its commitment to reducing extreme poverty by at least 5% globally. Our platform is designed for transparency, ease of use, and maximizing the impact of each donation.

No, Instaraising operates as a community development organization under the Peach Group. Our primary goal is social impact rather than profit.

To donate, simply create an account, browse through the list of causes or organizations, and choose where you would like to contribute. Our platform provides a secure and straightforward process for making donations.

Absolutely. Charitable organizations can apply to be listed on our platform. Once vetted and approved, they can start receiving donations and support from our global community of donors.

We conduct a thorough vetting process for every organization. This includes verifying their legal status, mission, financial transparency, and impact track record.

Donations made through Instaraising can be tax-deductible, depending on your country’s tax laws. We advise consulting with a tax professional for accurate information.

For more inquiries or to discuss partnership opportunities, please contact us at info@instaraising.com or visit our Contact Us page on our website.

Instaraising was founded with the vision of creating a more connected and impactful charitable ecosystem. Our goal is to harness the power of digital innovation to facilitate meaningful change and empower both donors and organizations to contribute effectively towards global welfare.

Instaraising serves two primary groups: individual and institutional donors looking to contribute to reliable causes and verified charitable organizations seeking support for their initiatives. Our platform is designed to cater to anyone who wishes to make a positive impact.

We prioritize transparency by providing detailed information about each charitable organization, their projects, and usage of funds. Regular updates and impact reports are shared with donors. Additionally, our platform adheres to stringent security protocols to ensure safe and transparent transactions.

Donors receive regular updates and impact reports related to the projects they support. These reports include details on the progress, achievements, and real-world impact of their contributions.

Yes, apart from monetary donations, Instaraising plans to facilitate volunteer opportunities and other forms of non-monetary support. This feature will be developed to enable users to engage with charitable causes in diverse ways.

We employ advanced security measures, including encryption and secure server connections, to protect your personal and financial information. Privacy and data security are paramount in all our operations.

Causes are selected based on urgent global needs and sustainable development goals. We categorize them into various sectors, such as education, health, human rights, etc., to facilitate easy navigation for donors.

Yes, we actively collaborate with corporations for their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Our platform can aid companies in identifying, funding, and tracking social projects that align with their CSR goals.

We encourage you to follow us on our social media channels and subscribe to our newsletters for regular updates, impact stories, and insights into our activities.

We value your feedback and suggestions. Please feel free to reach out to us via our website's contact form or directly at info@instaraising.com. Your input is essential for our continuous improvement.

We maintain strict oversight on fund usage. Each organization on our platform is required to provide detailed plans for how donations will be utilized, and they must submit regular reports on fund allocation and project progress.

Financial transparency is a cornerstone of our operations. We provide donors with access to financial reports, including breakdowns of how funds are distributed and utilized by the charitable organizations. This ensures that donors can see the direct impact of their contributions.

We conduct a comprehensive vetting process for every organization on our platform. This includes verifying their legal status, financial health, operational history, and impact track record. Only organizations that meet our stringent criteria are listed.

We strive to maximize the impact of your donations. While there are minimal administrative fees for the operation of the platform, we ensure transparency about these fees, and a significant majority of each donation directly supports the chosen cause.

We take any concerns or disputes regarding donations very seriously. Donors can contact our support team directly with any issues, and we ensure a thorough and timely resolution process.
