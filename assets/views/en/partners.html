Our partners

Instaraising is an innovative online platform that will change the way donors engage and how they can support a wide range of charitable organizations. As a new service, Instaraising will offer unique benefits for both charitable organizations and donors

Expanding the reach of charitable organizations

Instaraising aims to give charitable organizations access to a diverse and growing base of potential donors. This will be especially beneficial for various causes like environmental protection, education, and health initiatives.

Simplifying the donation experience

Instaraising is designed to provide a seamless and secure donation process, encouraging donors to easily support multiple charitable organizations. This user-friendly approach is expected to strengthen donor engagement and contributions.

Ensuring trust and credibility

The platform consists of a carefully reviewed list of charitable organizations, so donors can be sure that their contributions go to respected and effective foundations. This evaluation process is aimed at building donor trust in both the platform and the organizations.

Personalized search for charitable organizations

Instaraising aims to use advanced technology to recommend charitable organizations that align with individual donor preferences and past giving history. This personalized approach is expected to help donors explore and support a variety of causes.

Instaraising is generating significant interest among charitable organizations, as the service will improve how fundraising is done, expand donor engagement, and increase the effectiveness of charitable initiatives across various areas. The platform is expected to be a groundbreaking bridge between donors and a diversity of charitable causes.
