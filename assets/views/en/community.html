I’m an Instaraiser!

Congratulations, you are now an Instaraiser!

Congratulations! You have just taken a meaningful step in making a positive impact on the world. By using our web portal to find a charity organization that works with what you are passionate about, and making a generous donation, you have earned the prestigious digital badge "I’m an Instaraiser!".

What does it mean to be an Instaraiser?

As an Instaraiser, you are part of a community of empathetic individuals who believe in giving back. This badge shows your commitment to supporting causes that matter to you. But it's more than just a badge – it's a symbol of your dedication to creating a better world.

Share your badge

Share your "I’m an Instaraiser!" badge on social media and inspire others to become Instaraisers themselves. Your commitment to charity can inspire positive change and encourage others to get involved.

  • Stay tuned for more

    We are continuously working on new initiatives and partnerships to expand our community's impact. As an Instaraiser, you will be among the first to hear about exciting opportunities to make an even bigger difference.

  • Thank you for being an Instaraiser

    Your support and dedication make a real difference in the lives of those who need it the most. We couldn't have done this without you. Thank you for being an Instaraiser and for helping us change the world, one kind-hearted act at a time.

  • Get Started

    Ready to continue your journey as an Instaraiser? Explore our web portal, discover more charity organizations, and keep the spirit of giving alive.
